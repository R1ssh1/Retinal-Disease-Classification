Fundus image screening (demo)

Upload a retinal fundus image (PNG/JPEG). The model outputs probabilities for 8 ODIR-style labels and an explanation (Grad-CAM + text). Not a medical diagnosis.

Programmatic use: POST /api/predict with multipart field image, or JSON {"image_base64": "…", "use_llm_api": false}.
